# Day 11: Joins and Unions in Tableau

**Date:** February 11, 2026

## 1. Joins
Combining data from multiple tables based on a common field (Left, Right, Inner, Full Outer).

## 2. Unions
Appending data from one table to another (e.g., combining monthly sales files).

## 3. Relationships vs. Joins
Understanding how the logical layer (Relationships) differs from the physical layer (Joins).

## Summary
Combining data sources effectively allows for deeper analysis across different business areas.
