# Day 7: Dimensions and Measures

**Date:** February 7, 2026

## 1. Dimensions
Dimensions are qualitative data, such as names, dates, or geographical data. They are used to categorize and segment data.

## 2. Measures
Measures are quantitative data, such as sales, profit, or quantity. They contain numerical values that can be aggregated.

## 3. Dimension vs. Measure
The fundamental difference in how Tableau treats these two types of data.

## Summary
Understanding the distinction between dimensions and measures is critical for building any Tableau visualization.
