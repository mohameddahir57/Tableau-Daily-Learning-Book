# Day 8: Discrete vs Continuous Data

**Date:** February 8, 2026

## 1. Discrete (Blue Fields)
Values that are distinct and separate (e.g., Categories, Names). Adding a discrete field to a shelf creates headers.

## 2. Continuous (Green Fields)
Values that form an unbroken whole (e.g., Time series, Measures like profit). Adding a continuous field to a shelf creates an axis.
