# Day 13: Sorting, Grouping, and Filtering

**Date:** February 13, 2026

## 1. Sorting
Arranging data in ascending or descending order (Manual vs. Computed).

## 2. Grouping
Combining related items together (e.g., grouping specific states into Regions).

## 3. Filtering
Limiting the data shown based on specific criteria. Understanding the Filter Shelf.

## Summary
Sorting, grouping, and filtering are fundamental techniques to focus your analysis and highlight key insights.
