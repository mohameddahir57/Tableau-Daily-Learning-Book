## 1. Supported Data Types
- **String:** Text characters (ABC).
- **Date & Date/Time:** Calendar dates and timestamps.
- **Number:** Integers or decimals (#).
- **Boolean:** True/False values.
- **Geographic:** Latitudes, longitudes, and regions.
